House prices post crisis recovery

Prices in much of the Netherlands have still to surpass their pre-crisis levels. In the country’s four largest cities, however — Amsterdam, Utrecht, Rotterdam and The Hague — prices are now higher than at their pre-crisis peak, with Amsterdam prices furthest ahead. There are some regions where prices are still lower than before the crisis.
